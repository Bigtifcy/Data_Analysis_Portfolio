## üîç Insights

**Product Performance**

- Herbal Essence is the highest revenue-generating product.

- High-volume products do not always contribute the highest revenue, indicating pricing and profitability differences.

**Category Insights**

- Body Care dominates overall sales performance.

- Face Care records the lowest sales, presenting an opportunity for improvement through marketing or product enhancement.

**Market & Regional Insights**

- Asia Pacific leads market contribution, followed by Europe and USCA.

- Africa records minimal sales, indicating untapped growth potential.

**Customer Segment Insights**

- Consumer customers generate the highest sales volume.

- Corporate customers contribute strong profit margins relative to quantity sold.

**Discount & Profitability**

- Higher discount rates are associated with reduced and volatile profit margins.

- Controlled discounting could significantly improve profitability.

**Time-Based Trends**

- Sales peak toward the end of the year, particularly in November and December.

- A general decline is observed during mid-year periods.
